12-Week Filipino Meal Plan

Balanced • 2 Meals Per Day • Budget RM50–70/Week • Meal-Prep Friendly

← Back to Workout

How Your Meal Plan Works

  • 2 meals/day → Brunch + Dinner
  • Filipino, balanced, cheap ingredients
  • Weekly meal-prep to save time + money
  • Target calories: 1,550–1,700 per day
  • Rice allowed but controlled portions

Phase 1 — Weeks 1–4 (Foundation)

Build consistency, stick to simple weekly meals.

Week 1

  • Brunch: 2 eggs + 1 can tuna in water + 1 cup rice + tomatoes
  • Dinner: Chicken adobo (150–180g) + ½ cup rice + cabbage

Week 2

  • Brunch: Garlic fried rice + egg + sautéed sardines
  • Dinner: Chicken tinola (no skin) + sayote + ½ cup rice

Week 3

  • Brunch: Egg omelette + kangkong + 1 cup rice
  • Dinner: Ginisang munggo (with small amount pork bits) + sayote

Week 4

  • Brunch: Tuna + egg stir-fry + 1 cup rice
  • Dinner: Chicken afritada (light sauce) + vegetables

Phase 2 — Weeks 5–8 (Balance & Volume)

More vegetables, leaner proteins, same budget.

Week 5

  • Brunch: Tofu + egg + rice bowl
  • Dinner: Chicken stir-fry + broccoli

Week 6

  • Brunch: Sardines + egg + rice
  • Dinner: Ginisang sayote + chicken breast

Week 7

  • Brunch: Tuna omelette + 1 cup rice
  • Dinner: Pinakbet + grilled chicken

Week 8

  • Brunch: Fried egg + tofu + rice
  • Dinner: Chicken sotanghon (light broth)

Phase 3 — Weeks 9–12 (Cut & Tone)

More vegetables, slightly smaller rice portions for fat cutting.

Week 9

  • Brunch: Tuna + egg + ¾ cup rice
  • Dinner: Chicken tinola + more gulay

Week 10

  • Brunch: Fried rice + egg + kangkong
  • Dinner: Chicken adobo flakes + cucumber

Week 11

  • Brunch: Garlic rice + sardines + egg
  • Dinner: Ginisang ampalaya + chicken breast

Week 12

  • Brunch: Egg omelette + tofu + vegetables
  • Dinner: Chicken soup + ½ cup rice
