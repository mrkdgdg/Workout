Upper Body — Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

Repeat 3–4 rounds

  • Barbell or Dumbbell Bench Press — 8–12 reps
  • Overhead Dumbbell Press — 10–12 reps
  • Cable Triceps Pushdown — 12–15 reps
  • Incline Push-ups or Dips — 10–12 reps
  • Rest 60–90 sec

Upper Body — Pull (Back, Biceps)

Repeat 3–4 rounds

  • Lat Pulldown — 10–12 reps
  • Seated Cable Row — 10–12 reps
  • Dumbbell Row — 10 each arm
  • Face Pulls — 15 reps
  • Optional: Dumbbell Bicep Curls — 10–12 reps
  • Rest 60–90 sec

Lower Body — Legs & Glutes

Repeat 3–4 rounds

  • Dumbbell or Barbell Squats — 10–12 reps
  • Leg Press — 10–15 reps
  • Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift — 10–12 reps
  • Bulgarian Split Squats — 8–10 each leg
  • Calf Raises — 15–20 reps
  • Rest 60–90 sec

Core & Conditioning

  • Weighted Plank — 30–45 sec
  • Cable Crunch — 15–20 reps
  • Hanging Knee Raise — 10–12 reps
  • Treadmill Intervals — 30s fast / 30s walk × 8–10

Suggested Split (Hybrid Weeks 9–12)

  • Monday: Upper Body Strength (Gym)
  • Tuesday: Lower Body (Bodyweight + Dumbbells)
  • Wednesday: Rest / Core Mobility
  • Thursday: Full Body Conditioning (Bodyweight)
  • Friday: Gym Circuit / Treadmill HIIT
  • Weekend: Light Jog or Complete Rest
