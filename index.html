Daily Workouts + 12 Week Progressive Plan • No Equipment

MONDAY — Full Body

Repeat 3–4 rounds

  • Push-ups — 10–15 reps
  • Bodyweight Squats — 15–20 reps
  • Plank — 30–45 sec
  • Mountain Climbers — 20–30 sec
  • Glute Bridge — 15–20 reps
  • Reverse Lunges — 10 each leg
  • Rest 60–90 sec

TUESDAY — Upper Body

Repeat 3–4 rounds

  • Push-ups — 12–15 reps
  • Pike Push-ups — 6–10 reps
  • Incline Push-ups — 12–15 reps
  • Tricep Dips (chair) — 10–12 reps
  • Plank Shoulder Taps — 20 reps
  • Rest 60–90 sec

WEDNESDAY — Core / Abs

Repeat 3–4 rounds

  • Crunches — 15–20 reps
  • Leg Raises — 10–12 reps
  • Russian Twists — 20 reps
  • Plank — 45 sec
  • Hollow Hold — 15–20 sec
  • Dead Bug — 10 each side
  • Rest 60–90 sec

THURSDAY — Legs / Lower Body

Repeat 3–4 rounds

  • Squats — 15–20 reps
  • Lunges — 10 each leg
  • Glute Bridges — 20 reps
  • Calf Raises — 20–25 reps
  • Wall Sit — 30–45 sec
  • Side Leg Raises — 12 each side
  • Rest 60–90 sec

FRIDAY — HIIT

30 seconds work / 30 seconds rest × 3–4 rounds

  • Jumping Jacks
  • High Knees
  • Mountain Climbers
  • Burpees
  • Squat Pulses

WEEKEND — Running

  • 5km run OR
  • 3km jog + stretching

12-Week Progressive Plan

Phase 1 — Foundation (Weeks 1–4)

Goal: Learn form, consistency, moderate intensity.

Week 1

  • Follow the daily workouts as listed (3 rounds each)
  • Weekend: 5km run

Week 2

  • Add +2 reps or +10 seconds to each exercise.

Week 3

  • Increase to 4 rounds on Mon/Tue
  • Plank increases to 45–60 sec
  • Add jump squats to Thursday

Week 4

  • HIIT becomes 40s work / 20s rest
  • Add hollow hold to Wednesday

Phase 2 — Strength & Volume (Weeks 5–8)

Week 5

  • Add tempo push-ups
  • Add decline push-ups
  • Add leg raises

Week 6

  • Add 1 round to Wed/Thu
  • HIIT becomes 4 rounds

Week 7

  • Increase reps +10–20%
  • Add Bulgarian split squats

Week 8

  • Push-ups aim 15–18 reps
  • Add burpees

Phase 3 — Peak Conditioning (Weeks 9–12)

Week 9

  • Full body 4–5 rounds
  • Advanced pike push-ups

Week 10

  • Single-leg glute bridges
  • HIIT 45s / 15s

Week 11

  • Add 5th round to 2 chosen days
  • Add side planks

Week 12

  • Peak week: Max form + optional extra HIIT round
